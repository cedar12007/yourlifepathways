Non-Profits & Mission-Driven Organizations

Community-rooted, empathetic, and impact-driven.

I am currently offering a limited number of pro-bono 'Strategic Leadership Sessions' (3 sessions) to local non-profit and mission-driven organizations and leaders in the Washington DC Metro area. My goal is to support the DC community while expanding my portfolio in the mission-driven sector.

Focusing primarily on the Washington DC Metro area, I aim to empower those who dedicate their lives to helping others. In the non-profit and mission-driven sector, leadership requires a unique blend of empathy and strategic foresight. I provide an intentional space for mission-driven leaders to unpack organizational challenges, build effective leadership habits, and scale their impact.

These strategic leadership sessions are constructed to address the real-world complexities non-profit leaders face daily—from board management and resource limitations to team motivation and personal burnout.

Structure of the 3 Strategic Leadership Sessions

The engagement is structured to deliver immediate, practical value to mission-driven leadership:

  • Targeted Objectives: By the end of our 3 sessions, you will walk away with a clear 90-day action plan addressing your most pressing leadership challenge.
  • Executive Insight: We will tackle topics such as effective board communication, retaining top talent on a budget, and avoiding leader burnout.
  • Team Workshops: Beyond one-on-one sessions, I am available to host "Strategic Leadership" lunch-and-learns for your broader team, offering impactful insights to multiple members of your organization simultaneously.

As part of this pro-bono initiative, my only request is a professional testimonial or the opportunity to develop a brief case study detailing our success, allowing us to showcase the value of our work to the broader community.


If you are a non-profit or mission-driven organization leader in the Washington DC Metro area interested in these pro-bono sessions, I’d love to connect to see how I can support your mission.
