Educational Institutions

Mentorship-focused, academic, and supportive.

Each academic semester, I allocate time for two pro-bono Resume, Career, and Interview Workshops, and a select number of 1-on-1 early-career strategy sessions for graduating students.

As part of my commitment to community and mentorship, I work with educational institutions to provide specialized support for students and academic professionals. My focus is on delivering pro-bono transition and career coaching sessions that equip students with the practical skills needed to confidently step into the professional world.

Whether it's preparing for college interviews, transitioning from university to the corporate sector, or establishing strong leadership foundations early on, my approach is designed to be highly academic yet deeply supportive.

Practical Appeal: Resume, Career, and Interview Workshops & Coaching

To maximize the impact of our partnership, we focus on highly practical topics that stand out for students and young professionals. Potential session structures include:

  • Navigating the First 90 Days: Beyond the interview, how to establish a strong reputation, understand workplace dynamics, and build early career momentum.
  • Strategic Negotiation: Teaching practical negotiation skills for first salaries, roles, and early promotions.
  • Alumni Engagement & Keynotes: Pro-bono workshops or keynotes for alumni networks, bridging the gap between recent graduates and the professional working community.

If you represent an educational institution and are looking to bring practical, real-world career strategy into your academic environment, please reach out to me. Let's discuss how we can partner to help your students reach their fullest potential.
